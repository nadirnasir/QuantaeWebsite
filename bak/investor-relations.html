The Team

Company Overview - Quantae is developing the industry's most powerful adaptive foreign language learning technology solution; first release will be for the Arabic language. Our solution fits in the fast-growing language learning market to bring ease and effectiveness to an unprecedented level. Unlike a traditional classroom that presents the same material to every student, our adaptive learning engine continuously adapts to an individual's unique learning needs. This enables dynamic changes to the learning workflow to maximize language absorption to an unprecedented level.

Quantae is founded by a group of three close friends who bring together their rich technical experience as well as domain knowledge necessary to bring to the world the revolutionary concept of adaptive learning technology within the foreign language learning paradigm.
