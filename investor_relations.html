Company Overview.  Quantae is developing the industry's most powerful adaptive foreign language learning technology solution; initial release will be for Spanish and Arabic language, followed by French and German. Our solution fits in the fast-growing language learning market to bring ease and effectiveness to an unprecedented level. Unlike a traditional classroom that presents the same material to every student, our adaptive learning engine continuously adapts to an individual's unique learning needs. This enables dynamic changes to the learning workflow to maximize language absorption. Watch Video...
